divs:

The <div> tag does nothing on its own: it's just a place to add a class that is then applied to everything that is nested within it. It's very common to apply styles by using <div> tags to separate the content of your pages.

spans:

<span> elements are similar in that they do not have or need any attributes of their own. They are also useful in a similar manner for adding specific styles by adding classes, but they are used inline instead of block.

Refer to the block and inline element pages for examples of each!
